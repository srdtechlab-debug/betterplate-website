Privacy Policy for BetterPlate by SRD Tech Labs Last updated: March 2026 OVERVIEW BetterPlate is a nutrition tracking app developed by SRD Tech Labs, available as a free tier (with ads) and a premium subscription (ad-free). DATA YOU ENTER All meal logs, food entries, and nutrition data you enter are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any server or third party by BetterPlate. ADVERTISING (FREE TIER) The free version of BetterPlate displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to show relevant ads, including your device's advertising ID and general location. This data is collected and processed by Google, not by BetterPlate. To learn more about how Google uses this data, visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy You can opt out of personalised ads on your Android device by going to: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID PREMIUM SUBSCRIPTION Premium subscribers do not see ads. No additional data is collected for subscription management beyond what Google Play processes as part of the purchase. PERMISSIONS BetterPlate does not request access to your camera, contacts, location, microphone, or any other sensitive device permissions.
