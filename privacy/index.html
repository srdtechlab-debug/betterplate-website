01 — Core principle

Data you enter

All meal logs, food entries, nutrition data, body measurements, calorie goals, and any other information you enter into BetterPlate are stored locally on your device only.

This data is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party by BetterPlate. If you uninstall the app, all your data is deleted with it.

BetterPlate has no backend. There is no account to create, no login, and no server that holds your health data. Everything stays on your phone.

02 — Free tier

Advertising

The free version of BetterPlate displays interstitial advertisements served by Google AdMob (Google LLC). Ads are shown after meal logging, subject to the following limits we enforce in the app:

⏳

5-day grace period

No ads are shown for the first 120 hours after you install the app.

📅

Maximum 2 ads per day

Once you've seen 2 ads in a calendar day, no more are shown until the next day.

🕐

Minimum 8-hour gap

At least 8 hours must pass between any two ad impressions.

AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising ID, IP address, and general app usage signals to serve relevant ads. This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy — BetterPlate does not receive or store it.

Your nutrition data, meal logs, and health information are never shared with AdMob or any advertiser.

To opt out of personalised ads on Android, go to:
Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+), or toggle "Opt out of Ads Personalisation" on earlier versions.

To learn more about how Google uses advertising data: policies.google.com/privacy

03 — Local reminders

Notifications

BetterPlate sends local push notifications to remind you to log meals and maintain your logging streak. The following notifications may be scheduled:

Notification Time Condition
Breakfast reminder 8:30 AM Skipped if you use Intermittent Fasting diet; cancelled automatically when breakfast is logged
Lunch reminder 1:00 PM Message adapts if breakfast was also missed; cancelled when lunch is logged
Dinner reminder 7:30 PM Cancelled when dinner is logged
Streak saver 10:00 PM Only sent if no meal at all has been logged today; cancelled the moment any meal is logged
📍

All notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server to generate them. A maximum of 3 notifications per day are sent — the streak saver takes highest priority.

On Android 13 and above, you will be asked to grant notification permission on first launch. You can disable notifications at any time through your device's notification settings or from within the app.

04 — Device access

App permissions

BetterPlate requests only the permissions it needs to function:

🔔

Notifications

Required to send meal reminders and streak alerts. Requested on first launch on Android 13+.

🌐

Internet

Required only for loading advertisements (free tier). Not used for any other purpose.

BetterPlate does not request access to your camera, contacts, location, microphone, storage, or any other sensitive device permissions.

06 — Full disclosure

Data we collect

🏥

Health & nutrition data

Stored locally on your device only. Never transmitted. Includes meal logs, food entries, calorie goals, weight, height, and age.

📱

Advertising ID

Collected by Google AdMob for ad serving (free tier only). Not accessed or stored by BetterPlate directly. You can reset or delete it in device settings.

07 — DPDP Act 2023

Your rights

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

👁️

Right to access

All data BetterPlate holds about you is already on your device — you can view it at any time within the app.

🗑️

Right to erasure

Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data. For advertising data held by Google, reset your Advertising ID in device settings.

✏️

Right to correction

You can edit or delete any meal log, body measurement, or profile detail directly within the app at any time.

🚫

Right to withdraw consent

You can disable notifications in device settings at any time. You can opt out of personalised ads by deleting your Advertising ID in device settings.

BetterPlate does not store your Advertising ID and cannot process deletion requests for data held by Google. For data held by Google, please use Google's Data & Privacy controls.

08 — Paid tiers

Premium subscription

Premium subscribers (Poshan, Vaidya, or Guru plans) do not see advertisements. AdMob is not initialised for premium users, meaning no advertising data is collected from them at all.

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. BetterPlate does not collect or store any payment information.

09 — Age requirement

Children

BetterPlate is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

10 — Updates

Changes to this policy
