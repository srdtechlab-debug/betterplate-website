About BetterPlate

BetterPlate was created to make nutrition tracking simple, practical, and relevant for everyday Indian eating.

The Problem

Most nutrition tracking apps are built around Western food databases. If you try to track a typical Indian meal — dal, roti, sabzi, rice — you often end up scrolling through endless irrelevant options.

This makes calorie tracking frustrating, inaccurate, and time-consuming. Many people simply give up.

Our Mission

BetterPlate exists to make healthy eating easier for millions of people by providing a nutrition tracker designed around real Indian food.

Our goal is simple:

  • Make food tracking effortless
  • Support real everyday meals
  • Help users build healthier habits over time

What Makes BetterPlate Different

Indian Food Database

BetterPlate focuses on foods people actually eat daily — dal, roti, sabzi, rice, idli, dosa, poha and hundreds more.

Simple by Design

No complicated dashboards or overwhelming features. Just the tools needed to track food, calories, and nutrition clearly.

Built for Consistency

The best nutrition plan is the one you can follow. BetterPlate is designed to make daily tracking quick enough to stick with.

Privacy Focused

BetterPlate works without unnecessary data collection or complicated account systems. Your nutrition data stays with you.

Our Vision

We believe small daily habits lead to lifelong health.

BetterPlate aims to help millions of people become more aware of what they eat and make better choices — one meal at a time.

As the platform grows, BetterPlate will expand to include smarter nutrition insights, improved food databases, and tools that help people stay consistent with their health goals.
